Ask Us Anything

We have great answers for you

What is Teachlens all about?

Teachlens is a training or tutorial institute. We teach students of all levels from nursery to advanced studies and we also into mental development and diagnosis. If your child is performing below expectation in school, we can help you identify the likely causes and we will provide appropriate solutions. We also do brain mentoring aside diagnosis

How do I get a tutor from Teachlens

To get a tutor, click on Find a tutor anywhere you find the button on the home page. You will be taken to fill in short form and after that our representatives will call you for more information if need be. From there, we will recommend the best competent tutor to you

Where is your centre located?

Our offices are located in Lagos and Ogun state. However, we have virtual offices all over the federation. For Lagos and Ogun state addresses, please see our contact page

Are your tutors well-educated and well-behaved?

We have professional tutors who have been in teaching in Nigeria and British nursery, primary and secondary schools for over 5 years. These tutors are not flesh graduates or secondary school leaver. They are experienced teachers who are passionate about teaching and have been teaching for more than 5 years

However, if a tutor exhibit an inappropriate and unprofessional behaviour such as eating during class, joking instead of teaching, complaining, absent, asking for food etc, please notify Teachlens IMMEDIATELY

What is your students' success rate?

We have recorded fantastic success rates over a decade. More than half of students taken get above 90% in their respective exams and about 30% had above 80%. we've only recorded an average of 5 failed students for every 1,000 students taken. See our testimonials

Is your fees affordable?

Yes. Our fees are highly affordable. the charges depend on a number of factors

  1. Is the tutoring taken online or physical? Online classes are cheaper than physical
  2. The number of classes per week. The average number of classes is 3 in a week. If you book for less classes per week, the fee will reduce
  3. The number of students per class. if the total fee for your child's package is 50,000 Naira and the parent want to tutor to take another child. Meaning that the tutor will take 2 children at the same time, the fee will increased but significant discount will be given. In the case above, the two children will pay 75,000 Naira unlike 50,000 Naira for one child
